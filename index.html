Research

My research interests include graphical models, Bayesian inference and causal inference. In the last few years, I have been working on causal analysis on Chain Event Graphs, with a particular focus on developing causal algebras on tree graphs. I am also interested in Natural Language Processing and combining it with causal analysis.

I worked closely with reliability data with free texts, while my current research concerns more about human health.
