I recently joined London School of Economics and Political Science as a Research Fellow. My research interests include graphical models, Bayesian inference and causal inference. In the last few years, I have been working on causal analysis on Chain Event Graphs, with a particular focus on developing causal algebras on tree graphs. I am also interested in causal inference methods and their applications in public health.
